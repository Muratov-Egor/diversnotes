# Article Page

## Above The Fold

- H1 title
- Date
- Reading time
- Tags
- Optional series link
- Share / Copy link (minimal UI)
- Optional hero image

## Reading Experience

- Reading progress bar (top)
- Sticky TOC (desktop)
- Collapsible TOC (mobile)
- Anchor links on headings

TOC appears if:
- More than 3 H2 sections

## Content Blocks

Supported:
- Paragraph
- Headings (H2–H4)
- Lists
- Images (with caption)
- Blockquotes
- Callouts
- Code blocks
- Tables

## End of Article

- Next article
- Related posts (3–6)
- Subscribe block
- Comments (optional)
- Support/Donate (subtle)
