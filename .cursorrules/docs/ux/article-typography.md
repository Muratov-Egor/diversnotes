# Article Typography Rules

Контейнер текста:
- max-width: 720
- body font-size: 18 desktop / 16 mobile
- line-height: 1.65–1.75 (или 30px при 18px)

## Spacing between blocks

- p + p: margin-top 16
- p + (ul/ol): margin-top 12
- (ul/ol) + p: margin-top 12

Headings:
- before H2: 48
- after H2: 16
- before H3: 32
- after H3: 12
- before H4: 24
- after H4: 8

Lists:
- ul/ol padding-left: 20
- li spacing: 8
- nested lists: margin-top 8

Blockquote:
- margin: 24 0
- padding: 16 16 16 20
- border-left: 3px solid border (или accent-weak)
- font-size: 18 (как body) или 17, line-height 30
- цитата может быть слегка muted

Callouts:
- margin: 24 0
- padding: 16–20
- radius: 12–16
- border: 1px solid border
- icon + title row gap: 8
- title: 14/20 semi-bold
- body: 16–18

Code inline:
- padding: 2 6
- radius: 8
- background: code-bg
- font-size: 0.95em

Code block:
- margin: 24 0
- padding: 16
- radius: 12
- background: code-bg
- font-size: 14–15
- line-height: 22
- overflow-x: auto
- copy button: top-right, 36x36, opacity 0.8 hover 1

Images:
- margin: 24 0
- radius: 12–16
- caption: 14/20, text-muted, margin-top 8
- max-width: 100%

Tables:
- margin: 24 0
- font-size: 16
- cell padding: 10 12
- border: 1px border
- zebra optional (через bg-elevated)

TOC anchors:
- header anchor icon shows on hover
- anchor hit area: 28x28
